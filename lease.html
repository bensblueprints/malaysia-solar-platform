Common Questions

Lease-to-Own FAQ

What happens after the lease term ends?

After completing all payments (typically 8-10 years), ownership of the solar system transfers to you completely. You then enjoy 100% of the electricity savings for the remaining 15+ years of the system's lifespan.

Can I pay off the lease early?

Yes! You can buy out your lease at any time by paying the remaining balance. There are no prepayment penalties. Many customers choose to pay off early when they have extra funds.

What if I sell my house?

You have two options: transfer the lease to the new homeowner (subject to approval), or buy out the remaining lease balance. The solar system adds value to your property, making it more attractive to buyers.

Is maintenance included?

Yes, all maintenance is included during the lease term. We monitor your system remotely and conduct annual inspections. If anything needs repair, we handle it at no extra cost.

What credit score do I need?

We don't have strict credit score requirements. Our assessment considers multiple factors including your property ownership and electricity consumption. Most homeowners with stable income qualify.

What if my electricity needs change?

The system is sized based on your current usage. If your needs increase, you can add more panels. If they decrease, the excess energy is sold back to TNB under NEM 3.0, further reducing your costs.
