Our Story

From Vision to Reality

Malaysia Solar was founded in 2018 with a simple belief: every Malaysian deserves access to clean, affordable energy. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into one of Malaysia's most trusted solar providers.

We've seen firsthand how solar energy transforms lives - reducing electricity bills by up to 85%, providing energy independence, and contributing to a cleaner environment for future generations.

Today, we've installed over 500 solar systems across Peninsular Malaysia, helping families save millions of ringgit while reducing carbon emissions equivalent to planting 10,000 trees.
